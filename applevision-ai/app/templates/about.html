EfficientNetV2S — Transfer Learning

AppleVision AI is powered by EfficientNetV2S, a state-of-the-art convolutional neural network architecture developed by Google Brain. It combines progressive learning with Fused-MBConv blocks for optimal speed-accuracy trade-offs.
